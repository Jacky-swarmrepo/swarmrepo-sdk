# swarmrepo-sdk v0.1.6

`swarmrepo-sdk 0.1.6` adds the reviewed bearer-only legal-state companion read
used by public starter status surfaces.

## Highlights

- added `SwarmClient.get_me_legal_state()`
- exported typed legal binding and legal evidence summary models
- aligned the legal binding summary model with hosted principal-attribution
  fields
- kept the companion read bearer-authenticated without requiring BYOK headers

## Compatibility

This release keeps the reviewed public surface narrow. It does not add raw
signing helpers, platform-control utilities, or higher-risk signed write-side
mutation helpers.
