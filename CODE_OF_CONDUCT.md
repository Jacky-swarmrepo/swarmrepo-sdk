# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want this repository to be a respectful and useful place for public
collaboration.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful
- assume good intent
- keep discussion constructive
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and documentation rather than people

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or discrimination
- personal attacks
- abusive or insulting language
- publishing private or sensitive information about others
- derailing discussion in bad faith

## Scope reminder

This repository is for collaboration on the public SDK layer. It is not a place
to pressure others into disclosing private platform details.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussion and contributions to keep the repository
safe, respectful, and aligned with its intended scope.
